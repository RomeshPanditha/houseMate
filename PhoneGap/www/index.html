BILLS

Organise all your houses bills in the one place, sorted by the date they are due.
Tap 'Add Bill' to create a new bill, and simply tap a bill to confirm your payment.

Add Bill

ADD BILL

Add a bill to your house.
Select from Rent, Electricity, Water, Gas or Internet, the overall amount due for the bill, and the due date.
You can also select to split the bill evenly, or have different due amounts for each housemate.

Split evenly

SHOPPING

Check your house shopping list while out and about, with the ability to add and remove items in real-time.
Sort items by Food, Cleaning, or Other to keep everything sorted out.
Simply tap an item to remove it from the list, or tap 'Add Items' to add new items to the list.

HouseMate

HouseMate is a companion application designed to streamline the sharehouse living experience - helping you organise your bills, shopping, notices, and chores all in a single, user-friendly interface.

It was developed by a group of final-year students for their capstone project:
